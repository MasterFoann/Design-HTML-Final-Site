Intro

Welcome to Silly's 3D lighting section! Here, we'll discuss adding lights to your scene to make your render pop! We'll discuss your options for light objects, and methods of light.


Object Types

There are a few different light types in blender:


Point Light that emits from a single point.
Area An area that emits light, the shape can be modified in the properties panel.
Spot A cone shaped light, works similar to a stage light's beam, can be modified to be less or more harsh.
Sun Light emitted throughout your scene from one angle, used to simulate light from a sun.
HDRIs Though not an object, an HDRI can be applied to your scene, downloaded from external websites, to create very realistic reflections and lighting, but requires additional lighting for proper polish.
